2000s Romantic Comedies—A Standard of Love

Our idea of "true love" usually stems from what we have emersed ourselves in. Watching 2000s romantic comedies opened this door for me, and this ultimately flourished my own belief of love. Romantic comedy is a genre of film which portrays love in a light-hearted way, which people—including myself—grew to adore.


Reasons as to why I Love 2000s RomComs

  • Sense of Comfort : Due to its blithen portrayal of love, it gives an escape from reality.
  • Soundtrack: The choice of music is timeless.
  • Relatability: Some may find such films relatable, thus easier to comprehend.
  • Establishes hope: This provides another perspective of love, which is the perfect medium for hopeless romantics.
  • Entertainment: The lighthearted plot may be "corny" or "cliché" yet it is just the right amount to make people laugh.
